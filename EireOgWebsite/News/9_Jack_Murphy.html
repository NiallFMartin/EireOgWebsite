Player Profile - Jack Murphy

This week we hear from Éire Óg's 2019 Club man of the year, Jack Murphy!

1. Name?
Jack Murphy.

2. Position?
Full Back Line

3. When did you join Éire Óg?
2019

4. Where are you from?
Mallow, Co. Cork.

5. Favourite player of all time?
Seán Óg Ó hAilpín. (Obviously better known as a hurler but has a Munster Senior medal in football with Cork)

6. First footballing memory?
Going to watch my father play senior football for Mallow as a young fella and getting taken away from the wire for shouting “Dad Dad Dad!” trying to get his attention from the pitch!

7. First teammate you would pick for 7 a side?
Judging from out in UBC over the winter- Callum Roberts, number 1 pick overall.

8. Best player you ever played with and against?
Played with: Killian O Connor growing up with my club. (played with Cork minors for two years and played u21 level also) Played against: Colm O’Neill former Cork senior footballer in Junior championship at home.

9. First game in Croke Park?
Went to a lot of Cork games as a child in Croke Park, hard to remember the first one, but the 2003 All Ireland final v Kilkenny is the first standout in my memory.

10. Best Éire Óg moment?
I was going to say being awarded clubman of the year last year but overall, falling back in love with football after not playing for almost 2 years trumps any one moment.

11. Best bar in Vancouver?
Uncle Abe’s Main Street.

12. What’s on your Spotify playlist?
I’m a huge electronic music enthusiast. Also a lover of disco music. But my Spotify is filled with all genres of music!

13. Best holiday destination?
Love going to see new cities but if I was limited to one holiday destination for the rest of my life- anywhere with hot weather and a pool for me to lounge in would do the trick.

14. Favourite TV programme?
The Sopranos.

15. Last movie you watched?
Guy Ritchie’s Snatch. Was in need of a good laugh last week and it never fails.

16. Favourite match you've seen of all time?
Cork V Dublin 2010 All Ireland Semi Final.

17. Your county's best player of all time?
Larry Tompkins.

18. Best player in the game right now?
Pains me to say it as he’s a Kerryman- David Clifford.

19. How long have you been in Vancouver?
20 months this Sunday!

20. One must do place to visit in Vancouver?
Garibaldi Provincial Park.
