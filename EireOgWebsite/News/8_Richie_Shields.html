Player Profile - Richie Shields

This week we hear from the second most notorious Meath man in history since Pierce Brosnan, Richie Shields!

1. Name?
Richie Shields.

2. Position?
Half back line

3. When did you join Éire Óg?
2019

4. Where are you from?
Nobber, Co. Meath.

5. Favourite player of all time?
Sean Cavanagh , not tyrones biggest fan but he is a class baller.

6. First footballing memory?
Getting a pair of classic Diadora boots for u10 training.

7. First teammate you would pick for 7 a side?
Conor ryan if he was still around and fit!

8. Best player you ever played with and against?
Best player played with is Brian Farrell. Against Paul flynn from fingallians, class act.

9. First game in Croke Park?
Meath game in croker in 1999, Meath v Offaly.

10. Best Éire Óg moment?
Getting first win after joining the club.

11. Best bar in Vancouver?
Good night in Irish heather followed by the blarney after! Great combo.

12. What’s on your Spotify playlist?
Ah youd find anything on there from rap to country.

13. Best holiday destination?
Maui island, Hawaii.

14. Favourite TV programme?
Game of thrones , Suits , easy watch Brooklyn 99.

15. Last movie you watched?
Shot Caller.

16. Favourite match you've seen of all time?
Meath v louth in qualifers years ago in navan. Geraghty scored a last min winner, place errupted. Geraghty was flown in on a helicopter from a wedding then ran off pitch after scoring the goal straight back onto the helicopter to go back to the wedding!

17. Your county's best player of all time?
Few good players back in 90s and 00s but gonna go with Trevor Giles.

18. Best player in the game right now?
I think David clifford is unmarkable on his day.

19. How long have you been in Vancouver?
A year and a half.

20. One must do place to visit in Vancouver?
Deep cove, nice town just outside Vancouver with a nice hike to do aswell.
