Player Profile - Ben Kenny

This week we hear from one of our Lusk contingency, Callum Roberts!

1. Name?
Callum Roberts.

2. Position?
Half back.

3. When did you join Éire Óg?
March 2019.

4. Where are you from?
Lusk, Co. Dublin.

5. Favourite player of all time?
Paul Flynn - could do it all didn't score as much as others but worked harder than anyone.

6. First footballing memory?
Playinf for Lusk National School against our rivals Rush.

7. First teammate you would pick for 7 a side?
Conor Brick aka The flying Pig!

8. Best player you ever played with and against?
Played with: Cormac Howley of Round Towers Lusk best I've played with all around complete player and a good lad.
Played against: Probably Paul Flynn again, sent me across the pitch with a shoulder so I'll say him.

9. First game in Croke Park?
Dublin V Tyrone 2005, Mulligan's fake handpass game. Done 2 defenders and buried it, sad at the time but what a goal.

10. Best Éire Óg moment?
Western Canadian tournament in Edmonton. We didn't win but had some great battles with the best teammates. Road tripped up to Edmonton too which didn't help the football but won't be forgotten for a long time.

11. Best bar in Vancouver?
The Yard on Granville, can't beat the local, or the Irish Heather in Gastown.

12. What’s on your Spotify playlist?
A bit of everything. I'd listen to Paolo Nutini, Jack Johnson, The Wolfe Tones and Jay-Z quite a bit.

13. Best holiday destination?
Hard to pick just one but probably Hawaii.

14. Favourite TV programme?
Prison Break is my all time favourite watching a lot of Modern Family at the moment.

15. Last movie you watched?
Deadpool 2 good flick and shows a lot of nice spots around Vancouver.

16. Favourite match you've seen of all time?
Dublin v Kerry 2016 all Ireland semi final. Dublin were unstoppable in the last 10 minutes was great to watch as a Dubs fan.

17. Your county's best player of all time?
Cluxton or Alan Brogan for me. I’ll go Brogan just loved watching him play.

18. Best player in the game right now?
Jack McCaffrey just too quick for anyone and always shows up on All Ireland Final day.

19. How long have you been in Vancouver?
A year and a half now.

20. One must do place to visit in Vancouver?
Hike Panorama Ridge takes around 12-14 hours and it’s a tough climb but the view at the end is worth it.
