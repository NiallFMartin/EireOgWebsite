Spring League Finishes off well

The Spring league concluded with 2 great games between Éire Óg and ISSC

To finish off the Spring league Éire Óg and ISSC played out some great football on the night. In the first game events were nip and tuck right to the finish, finishing with a 1 point victory for ISSC Leinster, 1-11 to Éire Óg's 1-10

Éire Óg's 2nd game against ISSC Ulster was also another grea affair, however a strong start to the 2nd half for ISSC provided the platform for the win, to eventually finish with a score of 4-13 to Éire Óg's 2-14.

Thanks again to everyone for coming out, after a very enjoyable Spring league, the serious business begins on the 1st of June for the Vancouver championships.
