Huge win gets Éire Óg's Championship season up and running

The first round of the Pomerleau Vancouver GAA championship took place yesterday at Burnaby Lakes on a very warm Saturday.

We had a narrow loss for our junior team in a back and forth game against Fraser Valley Gaels, where a failure to take advantage of multiple goal chances in the first half were our undoing. Two quick goals conceded just after half time gave us a mountain to climb, leaving us 9 points down with 20 minutes left. However the team never gave up and by injury time the deficit was back to a single point. But in the end Fraser Valley managed to get an insurance point to win by 2.

Our senior team had a very convincing win in a game where we scored seven goals and a performance where everything seemed to go right.

Results: Junior - Eire Og 1-12 Fraser Valley Gaels 3-8
Senior - Eire Og 7-19 ISSC Vancouver Celts 0-4

Thanks again to everyone for coming out, round 2 of the championship takes place on the 15th of June.
