Spring League Round 2 Roundup

The Spring league continued with some more fantastic games throughout the night.

Éire Óg's first action of the night was against great rivals Fraser Valley Gaels. This game was no different with another tight game. It looked like Fraser Valley were going to win comfortably with a few minutes to go but Éire Óg pulled out a great win scoring the final 4 points of the game to win 3-07 to 2-09.

Éire Óg's 2nd game against ISSC Ulster was also another great nip and tuck affair with a single point seperating the teams with 5 minutes to go, but ISSC finished strongly with the last 3 points of the game to beat Éire Óg 1-16 to 2-09.

Thanks again to everyone for coming out, we look forward to next week to finish off the group stages of the 2019 spring league.
