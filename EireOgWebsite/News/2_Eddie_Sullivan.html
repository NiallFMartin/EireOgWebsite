Player Profile - Darren McAndrew

To kick off a new series on the website, we get to know one of our club's founding members and goalscoring extrodinaire, Darren McAndrew!

1. Name?
Eddie Sullivan.

2. Position?
Former goalkeeper, now selector.

3. When did you join Eire Og?
2018.

4. Where are you from?
Ballybay, Co. Monaghan.

5. Favourite player of all time?
Paul Finlay.

6. First footballing memory?
Togging out for our rival club's U-8's team. Not sure what that was about.I later transferred to Ballybay for a left handed Mikasa glove so it was a win-win for everyone really.

7. First teammate you would pick for 7 a side?
Russy Cleere.

8. Best player you ever played with and against?
With: Paul Finlay. Against: Jonathan Douglas or Tommy Freeman.

9. First game in Croke Park?
Down V Kerry All Ireland SFC semi final 1991.

10. Best Eire Og moment?
Beating FVG in 2018.

11. Best bar in Vancouver?
Orange No. 5, really good food!

12. What’s on your Spotify playlist?
90s Metal.

13. Best holiday destination?
California.

14. Favourite TV programme?
Curb Your Enthusiasm or The Simpsons(season 3-9).

15. Last movie you watched?
Back to the Future II.

16. Favourite match you've seen of all time?
Ballybay Vs Latton, Monaghan SFC semi-final 2012.

17. Your counties best player of all time?
Eugene 'Nudie' Hughes.

18. Best player in the game right now?
Shane Walsh.

19. How long have you been in Vancouver?
4 years.

20. One must do place to visit in Vancouver?
Deep Cove, go for the donuts, leave because you can't find parking.
