Player Profile - Niall Martin

For part 4, we talk to the club's website designer extraordinaire/okay defender, Niall Martin!

1. Name?
Niall Martin.

2. Position?
Corner-Back or Wing-Back.

3. When did you join Éire Óg?
August 2018, very last game of the year.

4. Where are you from?
Magheracloone, Co. Monaghan.

5. Favourite player of all time?
Tommy Freeman.

6. First footballing memory?
First ever game under 10s game that was reffed by Pat McEnaney, no memory of who it was against though!

7. First teammate you would pick for 7 a side?
Cian Murphy, rock solid defensive partnership!

8. Best player you ever played with and against?
With: Mark Cummins, Waterford. Against: Ollie Lyons, Kildare

9. First game in Croke Park?
Monaghan Vs Meath 2005. I'd say the first and only time there was a pitch invasion for a division 2 league final!

10. Best Éire Óg moment?
Winning Junior player of the year for 2019.

11. Best bar in Vancouver?
The Cinema Public house on Granville Street is pretty solid.

12. What’s on your Spotify playlist?
Mainly just Summer House music to listen to during work. Rofi James are a great band from home too.

13. Best holiday destination?
San Fransisco.

14. Favourite TV programme?
Silicon Valley, can hit a bit close to home with my own job!

15. Last movie you watched?
The Way Back. Good movie but very depressing.

16. Favourite match you've seen of all time?
Monaghan Vs Donegal 2013 Ulster Final.

17. Your counties best player of all time?
Conor McManus, his point from the sideline against Tyrone in 2018 is up there with Maurice Fitzgerald's.

18. Best player in the game right now?
David Clifford.

19. How long have you been in Vancouver?
1 year and 9 months.

20. One must do place to visit in Vancouver?
Skiing/snowboarding on Cypress Mountain is great craic.
