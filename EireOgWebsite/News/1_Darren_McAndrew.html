Player Profile - Darren McAndrew

To kick off a new series on the website, we get to know one of our club's founding members and goalscoring extrodinaire, Darren McAndrew!

1. Name?
Darren Mc Andrew.

2. Position?
Midfield before I got old/ full forward now that I am old.

3. When did you join Éire Óg?
2018.

4. Where are you from?
Arklow, Co.Wicklow.

5. Favourite player of all time?
Ciaran Mc Donald.

6. First footballing memory?
u12 SouthEast Wicklow Championship.

7. First teammate you would pick for 7 a side?
Billy Quinlan.

8. Best player you ever played with and against?
Kevin Hughes Tyrone / Leighton Glynn Wicklow.

9. First game in Croke Park?
1989 all Ireland final.

10. Best Éire Óg moment?
Beating FVG 2018.

11. Best bar in Vancouver?
Johnnie foxes / Irish heather.

12. What’s on your Spotify playlist?
What’s that!!??

13. Best holiday destination?
Cancun.

14. Favourite TV programme?
Ray Donovan.

15. Last movie you watched?
Rocky II.

16. Favourite match you've seen of all time?
Dublin V Mayo 2006.

17. Your counties best player of all time?
Kevin O'Brien.

18. Best player in the game right now?
Jack Mc Caffery.

19. How long have you been in Vancouver?
10 years.

20. One must do place to visit in Vancouver?
Indian arm.
